About Us

At Price Ahead, our journey began with a simple observation – the prices for sporting events were not always reflective of the conditions, and fan attendance suffered as a result. We noticed that, even in adverse weather conditions, ticket prices remained high, leading to suboptimal attendance.

Determined to address this issue, we embarked on a mission to create an innovative solution. Price Ahead was born from the desire to empower price setters with valuable insights into how weather conditions impact fan engagement. Our app utilizes historical data and cutting-edge weather analytics to provide accurate predictions on how different weather scenarios might affect specific fan bases.

With Price Ahead, we aim to revolutionize the ticket pricing landscape for sports events, ensuring that prices are not only fair but also responsive to the external factors that matter most to fans. Join us on our journey to create a more dynamic and fan-friendly sports ticketing experience.
